## Minification
- **Minification** is the process of **reducing the size of source code** (like Dart, JavaScript, CSS) by removing unnecessary characters.
- These characters include:
    - Whitespace
    - Line breaks
    - Comments
    - Sometimes even **renaming identifiers** (variables, functions, classes) to shorter names.
### ⚡ Why Minification Matters
- **Performance boost**: Smaller files mean faster downloads and quicker app startup.
- **Obfuscation**: It makes code harder to read, which can add a layer of protection against reverse engineering.
- **Efficiency**: Especially important in web and mobile apps where bandwidth and load times matter.
